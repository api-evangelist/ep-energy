# EP Energy (ep-energy)

EP Energy Corporation was an independent exploration and production company engaged in the acquisition and development of unconventional onshore oil and natural gas properties in the United States. EP Energy filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy in October 2019 and emerged in October 2020, with assets later acquired by IKAV in March 2022. The company does not maintain any public APIs, SDKs, or developer resources.
